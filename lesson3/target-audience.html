Tenki Southeastern Idaho

Target Audience

The target audience will mainly consist of three different groups. The first group and the majority audience will be those who reside in the tri-city area and those interested in the current weather conditions of the area. The second group will consist of those who may not presently reside in the area of Southeastern Idaho but have interests in the area. The third group will be all others who may not reside in the area or have interests in the area but rather may just be interested in the information provided by this website.

Scenarios

What should I wear today? What was the weather like 100 years ago? What will be the weather like tomorrow? Will I need to put on sunscreen?
